‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How to purchase Plus version?
A: Tap "Upgrade to Plus" in the app, choose Monthly Subscription (¬•8/month), Yearly Subscription (¬•48/year), or Lifetime Access (¬•98), then follow the prompts to complete your purchase.
Q: How to restore purchases?
A: Go to Settings page and find "Restore Purchases" option. Tap it and the system will automatically verify your purchase records and restore Plus features.
Q: Will my data be lost?
A: No. All data is stored locally on your device. Unless you manually delete the app or reset data, your data will not be lost.
Q: How to export data?
A: Plus users can go to Settings page and use "Data Export" feature to export focus statistics in CSV or Excel format.
Q: Which devices are supported?
A: Supports iPhone and iPad, requires iOS 15.0 or higher.
Q: How to create custom task types?
A: Plus users can create custom task types in Task Settings page, supporting up to 20 custom types.
Q: How to enable Elite Focus Mode?
A: Plus users can enable "Elite Focus Mode" in Settings. The system monitors app switching behavior and deducts points based on switch duration (short switches deduct 5 points, medium switches deduct 10 points, long switches deduct 20 points) to encourage focus.
Q: How to switch themes?
A: In the Settings page, select "Theme Selection" to choose from different themes, including Classical Courtyard, Celadon Glow, Lake Moon, Purple Mist, Peach Rain, Apricot Sunset, etc.
